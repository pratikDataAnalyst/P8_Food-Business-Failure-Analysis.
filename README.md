# Food Business Failure Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes a food business that generated revenue but still faced an overall business loss. The analysis identifies major operational and business challenges using Python, SQL, Excel, Power BI, and Tableau.

---

## Business Objective
To identify the factors responsible for operational inefficiency, revenue leakage, and overall business loss in a food business environment.

---

## Tools Used
- Python
- SQL
- Excel
- Power BI
- Tableau

---

## Key Insights
- Weekend sales were higher than weekday sales.
- Misal Pav generated the highest revenue.
- Food wastage significantly affected profitability.
- Offline sales performed better than online platforms.
- Despite generating revenue, the business faced an overall net loss.

---

## Business Problems Identified
- High food wastage
- Low weekday demand
- Operational expenses
- Platform commission losses
- Rural customer limitations

---

## Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards
- Monthly Trend Analysis
- Product-wise Revenue
- Wastage Analysis
- Platform Analysis
- Weather Analysis
- Interactive Filters

---

## Business Recommendations
- Reduce food wastage through better inventory planning.
- Improve weekday marketing strategies.
- Optimize operational and delivery costs.
- Expand operations in higher customer traffic locations to improve profitability.
- Improve online platform profitability through pricing optimization.
